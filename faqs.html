FAQs

1. What is the admission process for new students?

The admission process involves completing an application form, submitting necessary documents, and attending an entrance assessment or interview if required. For detailed instructions and deadlines, please visit our Admissions page or contact our office.

2. What are the school hours?

Our school operates from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Specific timings for different grades may vary. Please refer to the school calendar for any updates or changes.

3. What facilities does the school offer to students?

We provide a range of facilities including school bus services for safe transportation, CCTV monitoring for enhanced security, and well-maintained indoor and outdoor games facilities to promote physical activity. Our building is well-furnished, safe, and airy, with affordable fees and a well-organized computer lab.

4. How does the school ensure student safety?

Student safety is a top priority, with measures such as CCTV monitoring, secure entry points, and regular safety drills. Our facilities are designed to provide a safe and secure learning environment.

5. Are there extracurricular activities available?

Yes, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, music, and other clubs. These activities are aimed at helping students explore their interests and talents outside of the classroom.

6. How can I stay updated on my child's progress?

Parents can stay informed through regular progress reports, parent-teacher meetings, and our online portal, which provides access to grades, attendance records, and other important updates.

7. What is the school's policy on fees?

We maintain an affordable fee structure and provide information on financial aid and scholarships to support families. Detailed fee schedules and financial aid options are available on our website or through the admissions office.

8. What values does the school promote?

Our school emphasizes academic excellence, holistic development, and the teaching of Indian values. We aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared for future success.

9. How can parents get involved in school activities?

Parents are encouraged to get involved through various volunteer opportunities, school events, and parent-teacher associations. Contact the school office to find out more about how you can contribute to our community.

10. What support services are available for students?

We offer various support services, including academic counseling, special education resources, and mental health support. Our goal is to ensure every student has the resources they need to succeed.

11. How can I contact the school for further questions?

You can reach us by phone, email, or by visiting our office in person. Our contact details are available on the Contact Us page of our website

The Sanskar Valley School in Jaipur offers co-education from Creche to XII, with English as the medium of instruction. Affiliated with CBSE, New Delhi, we provide Science and Commerce streams at the senior secondary level, boasting a trained faculty and around 1100 students. Located in Bindayaka, we prioritize quality education in a supportive environment.
